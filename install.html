Safari Install Guide
Takes less than 10 seconds!
1
Open in Safari
Make sure you're viewing this page in Safari on your iPhone or iPad — not Chrome or another browser.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom centre of Safari — a box with an arrow pointing up. Tap it!
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen" 🏠 — usually in the second row of icons.
4
Tap "Add" to confirm
A preview appears — hit "Add" in the top right. Done! The app icon appears on your home screen 🎊
Look for this button at the bottom of Safari:
Share → Add to Home Screen
